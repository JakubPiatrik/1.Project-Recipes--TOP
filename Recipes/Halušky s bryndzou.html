Halusky

This easy Lasagna Recipe is meaty, cheesy, and packed with flavor in every bite. Homemade lasagna is better than any restaurant version and it feeds a crowd for way less than going out to eat. We love lasagna around here and have gotten creative over the years with Lasagna Soup and even Chicken Lasagna, but we’ve heard again and again from our readers that this is the best lasagna recipe they’ve ever made.

Ingredients

  • 600 g zemiak
  • 300 g hladká múka
  • 200 g bryndza
  • 100 g údená slanina

Steps

  1. Zemiaky očistíme, umyjeme, postrúhame a osolíme.
  2. V miske ich zmiešame s múkou. Cesto by sa malo oddeľovať od misky.
